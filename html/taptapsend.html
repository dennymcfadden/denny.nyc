Taptap Send

Taptap Send branding by Virgina Sasser

In the past few years, mobile money has become the predominant banking system for people living in emerging markets. However, transferring money from traditional financial institutions to developing countries is an expensive and inconvenient process. Thatâ€™s where Taptap Send comes in. Taptap Send is the first consumer product from Segovia Technology, a financial services provider backed by Reid Hoffman. Taptap Send allows users to make instant, no fee transfers from a debit account to a mobile money account.

I partnered with the Taptap Send team for 4 months in 2018 to launch the iOS & Android apps in the UK & France. Our primary audiences for launch were Zambian and Ivorian immigrants (living in the UK & France) who wished to send money back home.

As an embedded member of the team I worked closely with product managers and lawyers to define requirements. I also partnered with a user researcher, based in the UK, who collected feedback from immigrant communities on early prototypes.

Using React Native allowed our team to ship iOS & Android apps in rapid succession thanks to cross platform components.

Taptap Send has continued to grow over the past few months, processing hundreds of thousands of dollars in transfer volume, expanding to accommodate international business transfers, and offering additional countries for distribution.
